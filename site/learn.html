Your Guide
Prof. Oscar
Professor Oscar
Resident Academician — XO_OX Deep Research Station

Professor Oscar has lived in the deep for millennia, studying every form of sound that travels through water. Eight arms for eight voices. He has heard every oscillator waveform, traced every envelope shape, and mapped the topology of every coupling type.

He does not believe in prerequisites. He does not believe in gatekeeping. He believes that sound belongs to everyone — and that the best way to learn is to touch a knob and listen to what happens next.

"I have met students who never touched a synth before. I have met expert engineers who had never thought about sound as a living thing. I teach both. I meet you where you are."
