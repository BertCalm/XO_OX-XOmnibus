Philosophy

Every instrument has a voice.
We give it character.

XO_OX Designs builds synthesizers where sonic identity comes first. Not feature lists. Not spec sheets. Character — the quality that makes you reach for one instrument over another, the thing that makes a sound yours.

It started with two creatures: feliX the neon tetra — bright, darting, electric — and Oscar the axolotl — patient, morphing, regenerative. The X and the O. The snap and the sustain. Every engine carries DNA from both.

01

Character Over Feature Count

Every parameter must serve a sonic pillar. If it doesn't make the instrument sound more like itself, it doesn't exist.

02

Dry Patches Must Sing

Before effects, before processing — the raw sound must be compelling. Effects enhance character; they never create it.

03

Presets Are Product

Factory presets aren't afterthoughts. They're the first experience. Every preset demonstrates the instrument's reason to exist.

04

Coupling Creates Life

Instruments don't just coexist — they interact. Cross-engine modulation means the whole is always more than the sum.

The Platform

XOmnibus
"for all"

The collected works of XO_OX Designs, unified in a single creative environment. Four engine slots. Fourteen coupling types. Over twenty-two thousand presets. The architecture becomes the instrument.

Gallery Model

A warm white shell frames each engine's accent color. Light mode default. The UI gets out of the way so the sound takes center stage.

Mega Coupling

12 cross-engine modulation types. One engine's amplitude shapes another's filter. LFOs cross boundaries. The coupling matrix makes harmony interactive.

Chord Machine

One note in, four voices out. Timbral chord distribution across engines — every chord tone synthesized through a different character. House music DNA.

Sonic DNA

Six-dimensional fingerprinting: brightness, warmth, movement, density, space, aggression. Find similar sounds. Discover opposites. Navigate by feel.

The Aquarium

Six ways to drown your sound.

Every engine in XOmnibus passes through the same water on the way out. Six master effects — each named for a water phenomenon — create the sonic environment where all forty-six characters live. The aquarium is where brand identity becomes audible.

feliX lives near the surface — bright, darting, neon-electric. Oscar lives in the depths — patient, regenerative, still. The Aquarium is the water between them. Every sound passes through both worlds.
