Design Notes — feliX

  • Blue-to-red gradient mirrors the real neon tetra's iridescent stripe
  • Cyan bioluminescent stripe pulses — the "neon" in neon tetra
  • Forked tail with flick animation — darting, energetic movement
  • Bioluminescent particles trail behind at large sizes
  • Eye has cyan reflection — connected to the deep ocean palette
  • Works at 32px (icon) through 256px+ (hero)

Design Notes — Oscar

  • Broad head with 6 feathery external gills (3 per side) — the axolotl's signature
  • Gills animate independently with gentle wave motion — each on a different timer
  • Big gentle eyes with double highlights — approachable, curious
  • Rosy cheeks with radial gradient — warmth and friendliness
  • Bioluminescent spots pulse softly — connected to the deep ocean
  • Professor variant: XO Gold glasses + pointer wand with glowing tip
  • Tiny toes on front legs — detail that rewards looking closely

The Duo — feliX & Oscar Together

The odd couple of synthesis. Chaos meets precision. Together they're XO_OX.

The Duo Dynamic

  • feliX darts, Oscar bobs — their animations contrast naturally
  • feliX is horizontal (streamlined, fast), Oscar is vertical (grounded, present)
  • feliX glows cyan, Oscar glows pink — the XO_OX polarity
  • Together they represent the full range: chaos↔precision, speed↔patience, fire↔water
  • Professor Oscar = Oscar with gold glasses + wand. feliX as the "lab assistant" who keeps surprising the professor
