About XOceanus

XOceanus is a free, open-source multi-engine synthesizer for macOS by XO_OX Designs. It brings 76 distinct character instruments into a single unified environment — each engine evolved for its own sonic depth, each one a complete form of synthesis with its own mythology, accent color, and personality. Where most synthesizers offer one voice, XOceanus offers a fleet.

The coupling system is what makes XOceanus singular. Fifteen coupling types — including gravitational, adversarial, topological, and symbiotic routing — let any engine modulate any other in real time. ONSET's drum transients can drive ORGANON's metabolic rate. OPERA's Kuramoto vocal formants can bleed into OUROBOROS's strange attractor. The result is sound that cannot exist on any single synthesizer, because it requires the relationship between two organisms to come alive.

XOceanus is built around an aquatic mythology: the engines are creatures from the deep ocean, each evolved for its pressure zone, each bioluminescent. The name comes from Olokun — the Yoruba orisha of the unfathomable deep, the keeper of what cannot be fully known. "XOceanus — for all" is the product motto. It is free, permanently, under the MIT license. No subscription, no trial, no upgrade gate. The complete instrument ships to anyone who downloads it.

Key Facts
Engines 76 distinct synthesis engines, each a named aquatic creature with its own DSP architecture and mythology
Coupling types 15 cross-engine modulation types — gravitational, adversarial, topological, symbiotic, and more
Factory presets 17,300+ presets across 15 mood categories, organized by 6-dimensional Sonic DNA
Mood categories 8 primary moods — Foundation, Atmosphere, Entangled, Prism, Flux, Aether, Submerged, Coupling
License MIT — free forever, no subscription, no trial. Source code publicly available.
Platform macOS — AU plugin + standalone. iOS AUv3 and VST3 (Windows/Linux) in development.
Format AU (Audio Unit) + Standalone application. Supports MPC XPN export pipeline.
Expression Velocity-to-timbre in every engine. Aftertouch on 76 of 76. Mod wheel on 72 of 76. 4 macro controls per engine.
Creator Joshua Cramblet / XO_OX Designs — solo developer
