A: Click-to-Place Orbs

Click "+" to open engine browser. New engine appears as an orb you can drag to position in the field. Orb size reflects voice count. Coupling arcs glow between connected engines. Click an orb to expand its parameter panel below. Vertical position = water column depth (mythology).

B: Auto-Arranged Dock

Engines auto-arrange in a centered row. Coupling arcs curve above. Click orb to select (underline indicator). Simpler — no dragging needed. The selected engine's accent color tints the entire lower panel. Less spatial, more focused.

C: Compact Hybrid

Engine cards as a compact strip (not spatial field). Coupling shown as a slim info bar. Parameter panel with tabbed categories (Macros / Voices / FX / All). Knobs tinted in engine accent color. PERFORM button switches to the full spatial field + PlaySurface. Best of both: quick editing in compact mode, immersive performance in perform mode.
