## Part 4 — Analysis

### 4.1 — Which Engines Scored Highest? Why?

**Top 7 engines (9.0+):** OXYTOCIN (9.5), OVERBITE (~9.2), OWARE (9.2), OBSCURA (~9.1), OUROBOROS (~9.0), OXBOW (9.0), OPCODE (9.0), ORGANON (~9.0)

**Common traits of 9.0+ engines:**

1. **The concept IS the DSP, not a wrapper around DSP.** OXYTOCIN: circuit time-constants are the emotional state. OBSCURA: physics of stiff string vibration is the synthesis. OUROBOROS: Lorenz/Rossler/Chua are not metaphors, they run the audio thread. Every high-scoring engine earns its identity by making the concept inseparable from the sound.

2. **Academic citation doing real work.** OWARE cites Chaigne and Doutaut 1997. ORGANON cites variational free energy (Friston). OXBOW cites Householder matrix for N=8. These are not decorative — the citations map to specific DSP decisions. Engines that cited sources without implementing rigor (like OCTAVE's Leslie Doppler) stayed at 8.0.

3. **Zero dead parameters at seance time.** Every 9.0+ engine had all declared parameters wired. D004 failures predictably push engines into 7.x range regardless of concept quality.

4. **All 6 doctrines PASS.** OXYTOCIN passed all six at concept-level (with 3 flagged for pre-build resolution). OWARE (post-fix) and OXBOW (post-fix) both cleared all doctrines. Doctrine compliance is a floor, not a ceiling — engines can clear all doctrines and still score 7.x (see OUTWIT's initial 7.9 with all doctrines PASS).

5. **Irreducible identity.** Each 9.0+ engine does something no other engine can do: OVERBITE's Five-Macro survival system, OWARE's 8-mode Akan tuning grid, OXBOW's chirally entangled Chiasmus FDN. The council consistently blessed engines where the identity could not be extracted and transplanted to another engine.

### 4.2 — Which Engines Scored Lowest? What Patterns Do Low-Scoring Engines Share?

**Lowest-scoring original engines:** OBLIQUE (5.9), OCELOT (6.4), OBESE/OBSIDIAN (6.6), ODDOSCAR (6.9)

**All five original low-scorers shared the same failure mode:** D002 + D004 combined failures. Engines without LFOs and with dead parameters scored in the 5.9–6.9 range regardless of conceptual ambition. OBLIQUE had a novel prismatic bounce concept but 6 presets, no LFOs, and 3 dead parameters — all three failures compounding.

**Kitchen Collection low scorers (7.5–7.9):** OVERGROW (7.5), OVERWASH (7.8), OXALIS (7.8), OTIS (7.8), OVERCAST (7.9), OGRE (7.9)

**Common traits of Kitchen Collection 7.x engines:**
- P0 bugs (dead parameters or uncoupled systems): OVERWASH spectralField dead, OGRE soil typology dead, OCHRE LFO2 dead
- Conceptually interesting but execution gap: OVERCAST's anti-pad freezing concept undermined by LFOs still running; OVERGROW's logistic growth correct but modulation depth insufficient
- A preset library that doesn't demonstrate the engine's extreme parameters (OGRE: no preset with tectonic depth > 3 cents when max is 20 cents)

**Universal low-score pattern:** The ghost council is most disappointed by engines where the most interesting feature is inaudible or unused. "The EcosystemMatrix is the most novel DSP concept in the fleet and it is completely inaccessible" (OCELOT, 6.4). "The instantaneousFreq variable is a spectral compass that no preset has ever used" (ORIGAMI). Presence of an innovative feature is not enough — the feature must be *audible in presets* at seance time.

### 4.3 — Most Impactful Blessings Across the Fleet

Ranked by downstream influence:

1. **B016 Brick Independence (AMENDED)** — The amendment (synthesis-layer interdependence permitted) unlocked OBRIX's Waves 2–5, enabling Biophonic Synthesis (B-Wave 4), Reef Residency coupling (B-Wave 5), and the BROTH cooperative coupling architecture. A single seance clarification unlocked 30+ new parameters across multiple engines.

2. **B012 ShoreSystem** — The only blessing shared across two engines (OSPREY + OSTERIA) and referenced as a fleet-level shared DSP pattern. Established the principle that engine-specific DSP can be productized as shared infrastructure.

3. **B002 XVC Cross-Voice Coupling (ONSET)** — Established the cross-voice coupling paradigm that directly inspired OSTINATO's Circular Topology Coupling (B018). ONSET's per-voice coupling outputs became a model for the OUROBOROS velocity coupling (B007) and later the OXYTOCIN TriangularCoupling design.

4. **B038 Psychology-as-DSP (OFFERING)** — Ratified 8-0, the strongest unanimous ratification in the fleet. This established the principle that behavioral science with verifiable DSP implementation can count as a D003 physics equivalent. Expanded the definition of what "rigor" means in synthesis.

5. **B040 Note Duration as Synthesis Parameter (OXYTOCIN)** — The most architecturally influential design principle since B001. Established that temporal articulation (not just velocity, CC, or pitch) can be a first-class synthesis parameter. Buchla called it "the west coast argument since 1963, finally made systematic."
