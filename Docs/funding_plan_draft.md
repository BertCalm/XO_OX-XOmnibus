# XO_OX Funding Plan — DRAFT FOR OWNER REVIEW

> Status: Draft | Date: 2026-03-18 | Author: Board D7 + Khan Sultan synthesis

## Principles

1. **Core XOmnibus is free and open source.** This doesn't change.
2. **No "free forever" promises.** Content scope may evolve with sustainability needs.
3. **Revenue funds creation, not gates access.** Money enables more content — it doesn't lock existing content behind paywalls.
4. **Transparency.** Users know what's free, what costs money, and why.

## Revenue Streams (4 tiers, smallest to largest)

### Tier 1: Patreon / Community Funding (~$500-2K/month target)

**What patrons get:**
- Early access to new engines (1-2 weeks before public)
- Monthly "Studio Sessions" — behind-the-scenes sound design walkthroughs
- Name credit in engine release notes ("Funded by patrons")
- Patron-only preset packs (1 per quarter, 20-30 curated presets)
- Vote on which engine gets the next retreat / expansion
- Discord patron channel with direct owner access

**What patrons DON'T get:**
- Engines themselves (always free)
- Existing presets (always free)
- OBRIX levels (always free once shipped)

**Tiers:**
- $3/month — Early access + credit
- $8/month — Above + monthly Studio Session + patron preset pack
- $20/month — Above + quarterly 1:1 sound design call (limited to 20 patrons)

**Why this works:** Blender raises $150K/month from patrons. XO_OX at $2K/month funds ~80 hours of monthly content creation, which sustains bi-monthly engine releases + quarterly Brix Packs.

### Tier 2: Premium Boutique Engines (~$15-25 each)

**What these are:**
- Hand-crafted engines that go beyond the free fleet in scope, polish, and preset depth
- Voice + custom FX + deep sound design guide + 300+ presets
- OSTINATO is the first candidate (communal drum circle, firelight character)
- Designed to feel like a standalone instrument that happens to live inside XOmnibus

**What makes them worth paying for:**
- 2-3x the preset count of free engines (300+ vs 100-150)
- Dedicated FX chain designed specifically for the engine's character
- Professional-quality sound design guide with video
- Priority coupling support with the free fleet

**Cadence:** 2-3 per year. Each takes ~6-8 weeks to build at quality bar.

**Why this works:** OSTINATO as a $20 standalone synth engine is competitive with Arturia/u-he micro-products. The coupling with the free fleet is the unique value proposition no competitor can match.

### Tier 3: B2B SDK Licensing (~variable, $500-5K/deal)

Khan Sultan's highest-value insight: the SDK isn't for 1,000 amateur engine creators — it's for embedding XO_OX DSP into other products.

**Targets:**
- **Splice / Landr / ADSR** — XOmnibus engines as in-app sound design tools
- **Music education platforms** — OBRIX as licensed educational module
- **Loop/sample vendors** — XOmnibus-compatible preset packs as a distribution format
- **YouTube education creators** — branded OBRIX tutorials with license fee

**What they get:**
- Commercial SDK license (MIT for open source, commercial license for closed-source integration)
- Integration support from Kai's team (Atlas specifically)
- Co-branded marketing assets
- Access to the preset pipeline (Oxport) for their content

**Why this works:** Education software is a growing market. OBRIX + Professor Oscar is genuinely differentiated. A single education platform license at $2-5K/year funds months of OBRIX content.

### Tier 4: XPN Expansion Packs ($5-15 each)

**What these are:**
- Curated MPC-ready expansion packs built through the Oxport pipeline
- Genre-focused: "Lo-Fi Depths" (OCEANIC + OMBRE), "Drum Machine" (ONSET), "Orchestral Textures" (OBBLIGATO + OTTONI + OLE)
- 50-100 rendered samples + programs + cover art + liner notes

**What makes them worth paying for:**
- Pre-rendered — no XOmnibus required (works on any MPC)
- Professional mixing/mastering on rendered samples
- Genre-specific velocity curves and kit layouts
- Curated by Guru Bin (quality bar is high)

**Cadence:** 1 per quarter. Oxport pipeline makes these efficient to produce.

**Why this works:** MPC expansion packs are a proven market (Akai sells hundreds). XO_OX packs are differentiated by the 44-engine fleet that no competitor can match.

## What Stays Free (Non-Negotiable)

- XOmnibus core (all 34+ V1 engines)
- All factory presets (11,000+)
- OBRIX Level 1 (core synthesis toy box)
- OBRIX training mode (Professor Oscar)
- Community submission portal
- Quarterly Brix Champion program
- SDK (MIT license for open-source use)
- All future bi-monthly free engine releases

## What MAY Cost Money

- Premium boutique engines (OSTINATO, future candidates)
- Patron-exclusive preset packs (20-30 presets/quarter)
- OBRIX Levels 2-4 (TBD — may be free if Patreon sustains, may be $5-10 if not)
- XPN expansion packs (rendered MPC-ready content)
- B2B SDK commercial licenses

## Financial Targets

| Milestone | Monthly Revenue | What It Funds |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| Survival | $500/month | Domain, hosting, basic tools |
| Sustainable | $2,000/month | 80 hrs/month content creation (bi-monthly engines + quarterly packs) |
| Growth | $5,000/month | Above + contractor support for OBRIX, iOS, community moderation |
| Independence | $10,000+/month | Full-time XO_OX development |

## Timeline

- **V1.0 launch:** Patreon goes live. First patron tier active.
- **V1.1 (Aquatic FX):** First patron-exclusive preset pack ships.
- **V1.2:** First XPN expansion pack ships ($10).

- **OBRIX launch:** B2B outreach to education platforms begins.
