## OddfeliX — ODDFELIX

**Gallery code:** ODDFELIX | **Accent:** Neon Tetra Blue `#00A6D6` | **Prefix:** `snap_`
**Thesis:** Percussive transient synthesis — the snap, the click, the dart of neon through water.
**Personality:** Electric, Darting, Alive
**Polarity:** Pure feliX

### Aquatic Identity
feliX himself. The neon tetra — a flash of iridescent blue darting through sunlit shallows. Every note begins with a pitch sweep downward: the snap of a tail changing direction. He lives at the surface where light is brightest and movement is fastest. He is the X in XO_OX.

### Coupling Thesis
feliX gives transients — sharp envelopes that drive other engines' filters, choke their tails, pump their dynamics. He receives slowly — pitch modulation, gentle LFO drift — things that make his darting less predictable. He is the initiator. He strikes first.

The hammerhead shark. A predator whose cephalofoil — that impossibly wide T-shaped head — is two separate sensory platforms fused into one animal. Each eye sees a different angle. XOuïe gives duophonic texture — two-algorithm output that is harmonically richer and more complex than any single-algorithm engine.
