## The Flagship Pair

These are the origin instruments of XO_OX. Every engine in the fleet is, in some sense, a variation on the conversation they started.

---

### OddfeliX — ODDFELIX

OddfeliX is a percussive transient synthesis engine modeled on feliX himself — the neon tetra, a flash of iridescent blue darting through sunlit shallows. Every note begins with a characteristic pitch sweep downward: the snap of a tail changing direction. He is bright, precise, and fast — a clean FM-style engine built for leads, arpeggios, and anything that lives at the transient edge of a mix. feliX is the initiator in any coupling relationship: he strikes first, sends sharp envelopes that drive other engines' filters and pumps their dynamics, and receives back in the form of slow drift and gentle pitch modulation. On the feliX-Oscar axis, he is pure feliX. The best single coupling for OddfeliX is OddOscar — the complementary contrast that split the XO_OX identity in two.

**Best use:** FM-style leads, transient-forward arpeggios, percussive hits with character.
**Top coupling:** OddOscar (ODDOSCAR) — FILTER + SPECTRAL, medium depth.

---

### OddOscar — ODDOSCAR

OddOscar is a lush pad synthesis engine built around continuous wavetable morphing, modeled on Oscar himself — the axolotl, pink gills breathing slowly in a coral cave. His wavetable sweeps Sine→Saw→Square→Noise in response to envelope and modulation, and a Perlin noise drift on the filter is his perpetual, biological breathing. He is warm, patient, regenerative — the responder in the coupling relationship, absorbing energy from outside and transforming it into sustained warmth. Alone, he is a world-class pad engine. Coupled with OddfeliX, he becomes the warm architecture through which feliX's transients move. On the feliX-Oscar axis, Oscar anchors the reef.

**Best use:** Pads, sustained timbres, slowly evolving atmospheric foundations.
**Top coupling:** OddfeliX (ODDFELIX) — or any percussive feliX-leaning engine whose transients can animate the morph.

### XObese — OBESE

XObese is a massive-width synthesis engine built from thirteen stacked oscillators — the whale of the fleet. When OBESE sounds, it occupies the frequency space the way a right whale occupies the ocean: not through aggression, but through sheer physical mass. The thirteen oscillators are thirteen harmonics of whale song, tunable, detuneable, and controllable via the MOJO macro's orthogonal analog/digital axis. Its hot pink accent (`#FF1493`) is the unexpected beauty of a right whale surfacing. It sits Oscar-leaning in the Open Water zone. As a coupling source, OBESE gives raw amplitude and width; as a target, it receives pitch and filter modulation that breathes life into its enormous wall of sound.

**Best use:** Pads requiring maximum weight, bass-register foundations, anything where width IS the sound.
**Top coupling:** OddOscar (ODDOSCAR) — amplitude coupling at low depth opens MORPH's filter as the whale breathes.

### XObscura — OBSCURA

XObscura is a physical string and stiffness modeling engine modeled on the giant squid — for centuries, a myth; then a reality more alien than the myth. Its `obscura_stiffness` parameter is the collagen in the squid's mantle; its resonance is the body cavity acting as a drum. OBSCURA needs excitation — without a short, sharp transient feeding its strings and resonant body, it is silent. With excitation, it sings from the deep in daguerreotype silver, producing sounds with genuine physical stiffness and body. It sits at pure Oscar, the abyss. Best used in coupling configurations where a feliX-leaning engine provides the pluck and OBSCURA provides the resonant body.

**Best use:** Physical resonance layers beneath leads, cinematic string/body tones, textures with real stiffness.
**Top coupling:** OddfeliX (ODDFELIX) or ONSET — amplitude coupling provides the excitation impulse that brings the squid to life.

### XObsidian — OBSIDIAN

XObsidian is a phase distortion synthesis engine modeled on volcanic glass — formed when molten rock meets deep ocean water, crystallizing under extreme pressure into something with a pristine surface and the sharpest edge in nature. Its crystal white accent (`#E8E0D8`) is the glass itself. Phase distortion creates sounds with that same duality: clean harmonic surfaces that can sweep to serrated distortion in a single parameter movement. It sits Oscar-leaning in the deep. OBSIDIAN receives amplitude and envelope coupling that pushes it between beauty and danger, and pairs brilliantly with warm engines that soften its edges or aggressive engines that weaponize them.

**Best use:** Crystal leads, phase-swept pads, digital-edge tones with classical harmonic roots.
**Top coupling:** XOblong (OBLONG) — warm amplitude coupling to soften edges; or XOverbite (OVERBITE) — distortion coupling to weaponize them.

**Best use:** Dual-voice wind textures, post-punk leads, emotional arcs that need a single macro to drive them.
**Top coupling:** OHM — father and sons; or ORPHICA (their mother) for the full Constellation family sound.

### XOptic — OPTIC

XOptic is a visual modulation engine — the fleet's most unusual member. It does not make pitched sound in the conventional sense; it makes rhythm visible, converting audio into light-as-modulation through its AutoPulse system. Modeled on bioluminescent plankton at the surface, OPTIC's phosphor green accent (`#00FF41`) is the exact color of nature's own LED. It lives feliX-leaning in the sunlit shallows, where the light show happens. Its primary function is as a modulation source: feed it a rhythmic input, and use its output to drive anything else. The practical chain is ONSET→OPTIC→any tonal engine, creating drum-synchronized modulation without manual clock routing.

**Best use:** Rhythm-synchronized modulation sources; synesthetic LFO replacement; coupling-chain intermediaries.
**Top coupling:** ONSET (source) → OPTIC → any tonal engine. Disable AutoPulse when using AMPLITUDE coupling from ONSET.

### XOceanic — OCEANIC

XOceanic is a spectral separation engine — the prism of the deep. It separates the incoming frequency spectrum into bands, processes each independently, and recombines them at different depths, the way ocean water separates light as it descends (red disappearing first, blue last). Its phosphorescent teal accent (`#00B4A0`) is the last color of natural light before the deep takes everything. OCEANIC sits in Open Water, equidistant on the feliX-Oscar axis. As a coupling target, it receives full-spectrum input and dissects it; as a source, it provides spectrally separated outputs. Pair it with wide engines like OBESE or OddOscar and let it reveal the layers hiding inside their sound.

**Best use:** Spectral processing and separation, multi-band evolving pads, revealing harmonic structure hidden in wide sources.
**Top coupling:** OBESE — OCEANIC receives the whale's full width and separates it into its component layers.

### XOrbital — ORBITAL

XOrbital is an additive synthesis engine — the fleet's direct spectral sculptor. Where every other engine works in the time domain (oscillators, filters, wavetables), ORBITAL works in the frequency domain: each harmonic partial is a satellite in orbit, individually controllable in level, detune, and envelope. Group Envelope System (B001 — blessed by Moog and Smith) allows macro-level spectral shaping. ORBITAL's unique coupling capability is spectral: other engines can reshape its harmonic spectrum in real time via the coupling bus. Warm Red `#FF6B6B`. It sits feliX-leaning. Best partnered with OWLFISH — ORBITAL goes up (harmonic partials above the fundamental) while OWLFISH goes down (subharmonics below it), covering the full spectrum between them.

**Best use:** Spectral pads, organ-adjacent timbres, crystal leads with precise harmonic sculpting.
**Top coupling:** OWLFISH — harmonic + subharmonic coupling creates the full frequency spectrum in both directions.
