# XPN Patreon Content Strategy & Calendar — R&D Spec

**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Status:** Pre-Launch Planning
**Author:** XO_OX Designs
**Scope:** Full Patreon tier structure, monthly cadence, and content calendar April–December 2026

---

## Overview

This spec defines the Patreon strategy for XO_OX — tier structure, content cadence, specific monthly drops, video format guidance, and patron milestone rewards. The goal is a recurring funding layer that mirrors the XO_OX brand: generous with the free tier, genuinely valuable at every paid tier, and community-shaped at the top.

Patreon should feel like an extended liner notes relationship — not a paywall, but a deeper channel for producers who want to understand what they're hearing and where it's going.

---

## Tier Structure

### Free (Public)

**For:** Anyone curious about XO_OX — no commitment required.

**Benefits:**
- 1 free XPN pack per quarter (posted publicly on Patreon + XO-OX.org)
- All Field Guide blog posts (full text, no embargo)
- Public changelog and release notes

**Design intent:** The free tier is the storefront. Every free pack should demonstrate the quality ceiling. Field Guide posts are the brand voice — keeping them fully public is non-negotiable.

---

### Listener — $5/month

**For:** Producers who want to stay close without a major commitment.

**Benefits:**
- 72-hour early access to all new XPN packs before public release
- Monthly free SIGNAL pack (single-engine, 20–30 kits, one cohesive sonic world)
- Access to the full Patreon post archive
- Monthly patron-only text post: design decisions, rejected ideas, a technique from the current build session

**Design intent:** This tier earns its $5 on the SIGNAL pack alone. Early access is a trust signal — Listeners feel like insiders before the wider audience notices.

---

### Producer — $15/month

**For:** Active producers who want XO_OX tools in regular rotation.

**Benefits:**
- Everything in Listener
- Monthly free FORM pack (multi-engine, 40–60 kits, cross-engine coupling patches included)
- Behind-the-scenes dev videos (5–10 minutes, unscripted, screen-capture format)
- Preset voting rights — one vote per monthly poll (what gets built next, which concept engine gets prioritized)

**Design intent:** FORM packs are the premium product. The dev videos exist to explain why the sounds work the way they do — they double as tutorials without being tutorial content. Voting rights are real and visible; results posted publicly.

---

### Architect — $35/month

**For:** Power users, collectors, and anyone who wants to shape the direction of XO_OX.

**Benefits:**
- Everything in Producer
- Monthly free DOCTRINE pack (flagship multi-engine bundle, 80–100 kits, includes coupling DNA patches, extended liner notes)
- Beta build access — new engines and features before any public release
- Quarterly 1:1 office hours (30-minute video call, scheduling via Calendly)
- Community vote counts 3x — one Architect vote = three votes in any monthly poll

**Design intent:** The DOCTRINE pack is the most expensive content to produce — it requires the full engine suite to be functional and coupled. Architects fund that work. The 3x vote weight is the governance hook: if 10 Architects want OUIE built before OPENSKY, that signal is decisive.

## Monthly Content Cadence

Each month runs on a four-week cycle. This is the default rhythm; special months (launch, collections, holidays) extend or restructure it.

| Week | Content | Primary Tier |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Week 1 | Dev diary post — what's being built this month, what was scrapped last month, one in-progress audio clip | All tiers |
| Week 2 | Free pack or preset drop | Listener+ |
| Week 3 | Behind-the-scenes technical content — how an engine works, how a pack is designed, one DSP deep dive | Producer+ |
| Week 4 | Community roundup + next month preview — poll results, patron shoutouts, what's coming | All tiers |

## Video Content Format

**Length:** 5–10 minutes per video. No padding, no filler. Stop when the content is done.

**Format:** Unscripted dev diary style. Screen capture of XOlokun running in standalone mode, with MPC hardware visible or audible in the background. One topic per video; do not combine topics.

**Production standard:** OBS or QuickTime screen capture, system audio for plugin output, built-in mic acceptable for voice. No professional editing required. Jump cuts to remove long pauses are fine. No intro animations, no outros with subscribe prompts.

**Naming convention:** `YYYY-MM — [engine or topic] — [one-line descriptor]` — for example, `2026-05 — OBESE — Mojo Control DSP Walkthrough`.

**Posting cadence:** One video per month, posted in Week 3. Bonus videos (seance recaps, pack design sessions) are unscheduled additions, not commitments.
