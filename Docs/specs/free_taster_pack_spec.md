# XO_OX Free Taster Pack — Specification

> Status: SPEC READY | Price: FREE (bundled with XOmnibus)
> 5 programs from 5 engines. The funnel from free synth to paid packs.

## Philosophy

Every XOmnibus user who also owns an MPC gets an immediate taste of what
XO_OX sounds like on hardware. This pack ships BEFORE the paid ONSET pack.
It is the conversion funnel.

## Contents

| Program | Engine | Type | Character |
|---------|--------|------|-----------|
| **Synthesis Drums** | ONSET | Drum | 1 all-purpose kit (Drum Kit Core) |
| **Analog Bass** | OVERBITE | Keygroup | Deep, characterful bass (BITE identity) |
| **Crystal Keys** | OBLONG | Keygroup | Warm analog keys (Bob's filter) |
| **Ocean Pad** | OCEANIC | Keygroup | Evolving timbral pad (chromatophore) |
| **Grain Texture** | OPAL | Keygroup | Granular ambient texture |

## Why These 5

- **ONSET**: Drums are what MPC users load first. Instant credibility.
- **OVERBITE**: Bass is the second thing producers reach for. BITE's identity is distinctive.
- **OBLONG**: Keys/chords are essential. Bob's filter warmth is immediately appealing.
- **OCEANIC**: Pads fill the frequency spectrum. Chromatophore modulation is a differentiator.
- **OPAL**: Textures provide atmosphere. Granular is trending and undersupplied on MPC.

## Render Requirements

- ONSET: 1 preset x 8 voices x 4 velocity layers = 32 WAVs
- OVERBITE: 1 preset x 21 notes (minor 3rds C1-C6) x 4 vel = 84 WAVs
- OBLONG: 1 preset x 21 notes x 4 vel = 84 WAVs
- OCEANIC: 1 preset x 21 notes x 2 vel = 42 WAVs
- OPAL: 1 preset x 30-second texture render = 1 WAV

Total: ~243 WAVs + 1 stem
