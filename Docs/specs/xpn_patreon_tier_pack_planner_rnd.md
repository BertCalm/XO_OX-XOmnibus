# XPN Patreon Tier Pack Planner

**Status**: R&D | **Date**: 2026-03-16 | **Author**: XO_OX Studio

---

## 1. Tier Architecture

Each tier is a promise, not just a price point.

### Signal — $0 (Free Community)
The front door. Not a demo — a real gift that demonstrates what the ecosystem sounds like.

- **TIDE TABLES** gateway pack (free forever, always the entry point)
- Monthly Field Guide posts (full text, no paywall)
- Community Discord access
- Announcement visibility for all new releases

Signal patrons are audience, not customers. The goal is presence, not conversion pressure. If TIDE TABLES is good enough, some will convert on their own terms.

### Current — $5/month (Working Producer Tier)
The workhorse relationship. This tier is for producers who use XO_OX instruments regularly and want a steady stream of quality material without obsessing over the deep lore.

- 1 pack/month (rotating engine focus)
- Early access to engine firmware updates (2 weeks ahead of public)
- Voting rights on next engine development priority
- Access to pack archive (all Current-tier packs after their graduation window)

The $5 commitment is about habit. The cadence is reliable, the content is production-ready, and the voting creates ownership of the roadmap.

### Deep Water — $15/month (Obsessive Tier)
For people who want to understand how the water moves, not just swim in it.

- 2 packs/month (1 Current + 1 Deep Water exclusive)
- Sonic DNA raw files — the `.xpn` source architecture, macro routing diagrams, parameter mapping notes
- Unreleased experiments and engine prototype recordings
- "Under the Water" behind-the-scenes: build logs, failed approaches, design decisions
- First access to Theorem engine concepts before they're announced publicly

Deep Water is the inner circle. The content here is interesting to people who make instruments, not just people who play them.

## 2. First 5-Pack Vault

These five packs must exist before the first patron joins. They prove the system works.

### Pack 1 — TIDE TABLES (Free / Signal)
**Engines**: ONSET + ODYSSEY + OPAL
**DNA Position**: Balanced center — feliX clarity meeting Oscar warmth
**Concept**: The tidal rhythm that structures everything. Drums that breathe (ONSET), melodic drift that rises and falls (ODYSSEY), granular texture like light through water (OPAL). A complete production starter kit in three engines.
**Why it works**: These three engines cover percussion, melody, and texture — a full palette. New listeners can build a complete track with one pack.

### Pack 2 — CARBON PAPER (Current / $5)
**Engines**: OVERDUB + ORACLE + OBSCURA
**DNA Position**: Oscar-dominant — warm, aged, prophetic
**Concept**: Tape memory, inherited wisdom, photographic permanence. The pack is about things that leave marks — dub reverb tails, oracle voice textures, daguerreotype grain. Designed for lo-fi, cinematic, and experimental hip-hop contexts.
**Why it works**: OVERDUB's tape delay + ORACLE's formant prophecy + OBSCURA's dark granular = a coherent sonic world with serious depth.

### Pack 3 — SIGNAL CHAIN (Current / $5)
**Engines**: OPTIC + ORBITAL + OUROBOROS
**DNA Position**: feliX-leaning — precise, visual, self-referential
**Concept**: Light, gravity, recursion. Signal flowing through a system that transforms it at each stage. OPTIC for visual-spectrum synthesis, ORBITAL for group motion, OUROBOROS for feedback loops that eat themselves beautifully.
**Why it works**: Technically oriented producers will read the engine combination as a signal routing metaphor. The naming earns its own logic.

### Pack 4 — WATER COLUMN (Deep Water exclusive)
**Engines**: ORCA + OCEANIC + OPAL + ORGANON
**DNA Position**: Full vertical span — surface to abyss
**Concept**: One pack that traverses the entire water column atlas. Each engine represents a depth zone. This is the flagship Deep Water pack — it demonstrates the whole mythology in a single download.
**Why it works**: Justifies the $15 tier immediately. Nothing like this exists at Signal or Current level.

### Pack 5 — DRIFT STATE (Current / $5)
**Engines**: ODYSSEY + OBLONG + OCELOT
**DNA Position**: Oscar-center — warm drift, physical resonance, feline unpredictability
**Concept**: Motion without destination. Wavetable drift, physical string resonance, and chaotic feline energy. For ambient, downtempo, and generative contexts.
**Why it works**: OBLONG's physicality anchors ODYSSEY's drift, and OCELOT's unpredictability ensures nothing sounds static for long.

## 3. Monthly Release Cadence

**Month 1**: TIDE TABLES launches publicly. No Patreon yet. Build the audience.

**Month 2–3**: Complete the 5-pack vault. Internal testing, sonic DNA documentation. Patreon page drafted but not live.

**Month 4**: Patreon launch. All 5 packs available on day one. Signal gets TIDE TABLES. Current gets packs 2, 3, 5 in the archive. Deep Water gets pack 4 plus the Month 4 exclusive.

**Month 5+**: Steady state — 1 Current pack/month, 1 Deep Water exclusive/month.

**During Concept Engine Builds (OSTINATO/OPENSKY/OCEANDEEP/OUIE)**: Content gap is real. Strategy: release "engine preview" packs — 30–40 presets from a single new engine, marketed as early access. These serve as both pack content and beta testing. Deep Water gets the raw Sonic DNA files from the build process. The build itself becomes content.

---

## 4. Pack Graduation Policy

Tiers are not permanent. Packs move down over time — this creates urgency without artificial scarcity.

| Window | Movement |
|--------|----------|
| 0–3 months | Deep Water exclusive |
| 3–6 months | Deep Water + Current ($5) |
| 6–12 months | Current archive (all $5 subscribers) |
| 12+ months | Free / Signal (selected packs only, curator's choice) |

Not every pack graduates to free. The free tier should always feel curated, not like a dump of old content. TIDE TABLES stays free forever. One or two other packs per year may graduate at the studio's discretion.

## 5. DNA Tier Mapping

The Sonic DNA axis (feliX ←→ Oscar) maps naturally to tier access.

| DNA Zone | Character | Tier |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Pure feliX — clinical, precise, bright | Gateway accessible | Signal / free |
| feliX-center — clean with warmth | Entry production | Current |
| Center / balanced | Core production | Current |
| Oscar-center — warm, aged, physical | Advanced production | Current / Deep Water |
| Pure Oscar — dark, abyssal, extreme | Specialist territory | Deep Water |
| Bipolar extremes — full STRIFE or LOVE | Experimental, raw | Deep Water exclusive |

The free tier is not lesser — it's positioned at the most accessible point on the DNA spectrum. TIDE TABLES sits at center-feliX intentionally: immediately usable, musically obvious, a true gift. Deep Water exclusives live at the extremes because those sounds require context to use well. The behind-the-scenes content provides that context.
