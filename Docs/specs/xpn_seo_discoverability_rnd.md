## 1. Search Intent for MPC Packs

Producers searching for MPC expansion packs cluster into three intent tiers:

**Transactional (buy now):** "mpc expansion packs free download", "akai mpc one expansion pack", "mpc live 2 expansion", "mpc drum kits download", "mpc one drum kit free". These convert fastest — buyers have their hardware open.

**Comparative (researching):** "best mpc expansion packs 2025", "mpc expansion packs worth it", "akai mpc vs maschine expansion library". These searchers are one good article away from a purchase.

**Instructional (learning + discovering):** "how to load expansion packs mpc", "mpc expansion pack tutorial", "how to use keygroup programs mpc". These rank well for blog content and pull in newer MPC owners who become buyers.

**High-value long-tail targets with buyer intent:**
- "mpc one drum machines expansion" — hardware-specific, low competition
- "akai mpc live 3 expansion packs" — version-specific, very low competition, MPC3 owners are active buyers
- "mpc expansion packs hip hop free" — genre + platform + intent
- "akai mpc latin percussion expansion" — genre specificity = less competition, clearer buyer
- "mpc expansion creative synth packs" — separates from the drum-only pack market
- "mpc expansion vintage drums" — nostalgia/genre intent converts well

For XO_OX specifically: "xpn expansion pack mpc", "ritual drums mpc", "experimental mpc expansion" are low-competition niches with growing demand from forward-thinking MPC users.

## 2. Gumroad SEO

Gumroad's internal search weights **title** first, then **description text**, then **tags** (limited to 10). Google also crawls Gumroad product pages, so these are doubly important.

**Title formula:** `[Character Name] — [Pack Type] for Akai MPC | XO_OX`
Example: `ONSET Ritual Drums — MPC Expansion Pack | XO_OX`

The title should front-load the most searched phrase. "MPC Expansion Pack" outperforms "XPN Pack" with outside audiences. Include the character name for brand-building.

**Description structure for ranking:**
- First 150 characters act as the meta description in Google results — lead with the core keyword phrase: "ONSET Ritual Drums is a 115-preset MPC expansion pack designed for Akai MPC One, Live, and Live 3."
- Use the words "Akai MPC", "expansion pack", "drum kit", and the genre (ritual, industrial, hip hop) at least 2–3x naturally in the body.
- List hardware compatibility explicitly: "Compatible with MPC One, MPC Live, MPC Live 3, MPC X, MPC Key 61" — these are searchable terms.

**Gumroad tags (use all 10):**
`mpc expansion`, `akai mpc`, `drum kit`, `mpc one`, `mpc live`, `drum machine`, `hip hop`, `experimental`, `xo-ox`, `ritual drums`

## 4. YouTube SEO

Demo videos are the most powerful discovery channel for MPC packs. YouTube is the second-largest search engine; producers search it like Google.

**Title formula:** `[Pack Name] [Descriptor] — MPC Expansion Pack Demo (Akai MPC [Hardware Model])`

Concrete examples:
- `ONSET Ritual Drums — MPC Expansion Pack Demo (Akai MPC Live 3)`
- `OPAL Granular Textures — Experimental MPC Expansion (Akai MPC One)`
- `OBLONG Bob Bass Character — Hip Hop MPC Expansion Demo`

**Avoid:** "Demo #3", "XO_OX New Pack", "Check This Out" — these get zero search traffic.

**Description best practices:**
- First 2 lines (visible before "show more") should contain: pack name, "MPC expansion pack", hardware model, download link
- List the hardware models in the description body: "Works with MPC One, MPC Live, MPC Live 3, MPC X"
- Add timestamps if the video covers multiple sections — YouTube surfaces timestamped content in Google search results
- Tags: `mpc expansion pack`, `akai mpc demo`, `drum kit demo`, `mpc one sounds`, `free mpc samples`

## 5. MPC-Forums Indexing

MPC-Forums threads rank in Google for long-tail MPC queries. A thread titled "New Free MPC Expansion — Ritual Drums by XO_OX" will index and appear when someone searches "free mpc ritual drums" or "mpc expansion drum machine character".

**What makes a forum thread rank:**
- Thread title contains the full keyword phrase — "Free MPC Expansion Pack Download" in the title, not just "Cool New Pack"
- First post includes the download link prominently and describes the pack with natural keywords
- Engagement (replies) signals relevance to Google — ask a question at the end of the post to invite responses
- External backlinks to the thread (from your site, social) improve the thread's authority

**3-post credibility series structure:**
- Post 1 (Introduction): Free pack announcement — title includes "Free MPC Expansion" + pack name. Body contains full description, compatibility list, download link.
- Post 2 (Behind the Sounds): "Making of [Pack Name] — XO_OX Engine Design" — attracts gear/sound-design readers and generates a second indexed page.
- Post 3 (Update/Expansion): After 2–4 weeks, post an update with new presets or a follow-up kit — keeps the thread active, refreshes Google's crawl date.
