## Overview

This document defines the concrete Patreon content calendar for Q2 2026 (April, May, June). Each month is built around one featured engine and one flagship XPN expansion pack release, with behind-the-scenes content laddered across tiers and Field Guide articles timed to the pack launch cycle.

**Patreon URL:** https://www.patreon.com/cw/XO_OX (live as of 2026-03-22)

**Tier structure:**
- **Free** — public announcements, Field Guide links
- **Supporter ($5)** — monthly sample pack, update post
- **Producer ($15)** — SIGNAL pack (30-day exclusive), BTS post, early Field Guide access
- **Architect ($35)** — DOCTRINE pack or early FORM access, live Q&A, engine spec doc, voting rights

## April 2026 — ONSET Month

**Theme:** Drum machine showcase. MPC-native performance kits. The axolotl in the machine.
**Anchor Pack:** *MACHINE GUN REEF* — ONSET drum expansion, Vol. 1 (8 performance kits, 128 pads)

### April 1 — Patreon Launch Announcement (All Tiers)

Post: "Q2 2026 Is Live — Here's What's Coming"
- Announce April/May/June engine features
- Tease MACHINE GUN REEF with a 30-second audio clip (kick + snare demo)
- Update Patreon URL in post footer — confirm the actual link is live before publishing

### April 4 — Architect Tier Delivery

- **DOCTRINE Pack:** *ONSET: Foundations* — 40 presets drawn from the ONSET engine's canonical synthesis voices. Architect-exclusive; never going public.
- **Behind-the-scenes spec document:** ONSET character brief — covers the axolotl identity, Electric Blue `#0066FF` accent, 8 synthesis voices (Kick/Snare/CHat/OHat/Clap/Tom/Perc/FX), 4 macros (MACHINE/PUNCH/SPACE/MUTATE), and the design decisions behind the 115 factory presets.
- **Voting post:** "May Engine: OPAL — which direction?" Three options: (A) ambient texture focus, (B) rhythmic granular, (C) melodic phrases. Voting closes April 18.

### April 7 — Early Field Guide Access (Producer + Architect)

- Deliver draft of Field Guide Article 1: "Why Most MPC Expansion Packs Are Wrong About Velocity" to Producer and Architect tiers — 3 weeks before public publish date (April 28).
- Post framing: "This is the intellectual case for how MACHINE GUN REEF was designed. Read this first, then load the pack."

### April 11 — SIGNAL Pack Release: MACHINE GUN REEF (Producer + Architect, 30-day exclusive)

Pack contents:
- 8 performance kits (LIVE + SEQUENCE mode variants per kit = 16 XPN files total)
- 128 pads across kits: Kick × 3 velocities, Snare × 3, CHat/OHat choke pairs, Clap, Tom × 2, Perc × 2, FX × 2 per kit
- Sonic DNA velocity mapping — soft hits Oscar-warm, hard hits feliX-bright
- Q-Link assignments: MACHINE → brightness axis, PUNCH → transient shaping, SPACE → reverb send, MUTATE → round-robin variant select
- `MPCE_SETUP.md` included for quad-corner assignments

Post title: "MACHINE GUN REEF — ONSET Vol. 1 Is Here (Producer Early Access)"
- Include setup guide, MPCE_SETUP.md walkthrough, and choke group map
- Behind-the-scenes post: "How I Designed MACHINE GUN REEF" — covers the rendering decision (which ONSET preset states were captured), the LIVE vs. SEQUENCE mode split, and why the FX voice got 4 velocity layers instead of 3.

### April 14 — Supporter Tier Delivery

- **Free sample pack:** *ONSET Transients* — 12 single-shot samples: 3 kicks (soft/medium/hard), 3 snares, 3 hi-hats, 3 clap variants. Dry, no reverb. Ready-to-use on any MPC or DAW.
- **Monthly update post:** "What Shipped in March, What's Coming in April" — bullet list format, 300 words max.

### April 28 — Field Guide Article 1 Public Publish (Free Tier)

- "Why Most MPC Expansion Packs Are Wrong About Velocity" goes live on XO-OX.org/guide
- Announcement post on Patreon (Free tier): link + 2-sentence summary
- MACHINE GUN REEF remains Producer/Architect exclusive until May 11

- **Early FORM Access:** *CHROMODORIS DRIFT (Architect Pre-Release)* — same pack as Producer SIGNAL release below, delivered one week early with an additional bonus bank: *DRIFT STEMS* — 16 long-form ambient renders (30-90 seconds each) from OPAL's most distinctive synthesis states. These stems are Architect-exclusive.

- **Monthly Q&A / video walkthrough:** 30-minute screen-recorded walkthrough of CHROMODORIS DRIFT — how each bank was designed, what the OPAL synthesis states look like in the plugin, how to use the OPAL→DUB coupling path even from a static pack.

### May 9 — Early Field Guide Access (Producer + Architect)

- Deliver draft of Field Guide Article 2: "The ONSET Drum Machine: Designing Kits for Pad Performance" — 3 weeks before public publish date (May 30). Note: this article is ONSET-focused but releases in May to maintain the 1-article-per-month cadence without front-loading April.
- Post framing: "The article covers ONSET specifically, but the design principles — rendering a synthesizer to samples, choke group architecture — apply directly to OPAL packs too."

### May 11 — MACHINE GUN REEF Goes Public

- ONSET Vol. 1 pack releases publicly (after 30-day Producer exclusive)
- Announcement post on Free tier + link to purchase
- Producer tier post: "Your exclusive window closed — thanks for supporting early. CHROMODORIS DRIFT is your next exclusive."

### May 13 — SIGNAL Pack Release: CHROMODORIS DRIFT (Producer + Architect)

Pack contents:
- 6 texture banks: Pelagic Shimmer, Deep Pulse, Grain Storm, Breath Cycle, Chromatic Bloom, Abyss Layer
- 96 pads: 16 pads per bank, 3-4 velocity layers per pad
- Round-robin variants tuned for non-repeating atmospheric playback
- Coupling annotation: every pad tagged with recommended OPAL→DUB coupling preset name
- `MPCE_SETUP.md` for quad-corner: NE = feliX grain (sparse, bright), SW = Oscar grain (dense, warm), NW = COUPLING (pre-routed for OVERDUB send), SE = SPACE (wet tail, extended decay)

Post title: "CHROMODORIS DRIFT — OPAL Vol. 1 Is Here (Producer Early Access)"
- Behind-the-scenes post: "How I Designed CHROMODORIS DRIFT" — covers grain size decisions per bank, the challenge of rendering OPAL's continuous grain field to static velocity layers, and why 6 banks (not 8) gives the pack more identity.

### May 14 — Supporter Tier Delivery

- **Free sample pack:** *OPAL Grain Textures* — 16 single-shot and short-loop samples: 8 granular swells (varying grain density), 4 rhythmic grain pulses, 4 melodic grain fragments. Dry renders, no FX.
- **Monthly update post:** "What Shipped in April, What's Coming in May" — include MACHINE GUN REEF public release note and CHROMODORIS DRIFT preview.

### May 30 — Field Guide Article 2 Public Publish (Free Tier)

- "The ONSET Drum Machine: Designing Kits for Pad Performance" goes live on XO-OX.org/guide
- Free tier announcement post with link

**Theme:** Character bass synthesis. Hip-hop weight. The Amber engine.
**Anchor Pack:** *AMBER WEIGHT* — OBLONG character bass expansion, Vol. 1 (5 bass banks + 1 performance bank, 96 pads)

- Tease AMBER WEIGHT with a 30-second bass demo clip

### June 3 — Architect Tier Delivery

- **DOCTRINE Pack:** *OBLONG: Character Studies* — 40 presets drawn from OBLONG's most distinctive bass character states. Architect-exclusive.
- **Behind-the-scenes spec document:** OBLONG character brief — Amber `#E9A84A` accent, `bob_` parameter prefix, bass-forward character design philosophy, feliX-lean identity (tight attacks, defined fundamental), and hip-hop production context (the XOblong origin story).
- **Monthly Q&A / video walkthrough:** 30-minute session on bass pack design — how to layer OBLONG pads with ONSET kick pads, Q-Link performance on AMBER WEIGHT, and a live MPC beat sketch using both packs together.

### June 7 — Early Field Guide Access (Producer + Architect)

- Deliver draft of Field Guide Article 3: "MPCe Native Design: The Untapped Frontier" — 3 weeks before public publish date (June 28).
- Post framing: "AMBER WEIGHT is designed with MPCe quad-corners from the ground up. This article explains what that means and how to use the NW/NE/SW/SE corner assignments in practice."

### June 10 — CHROMODORIS DRIFT Goes Public

- OPAL Vol. 1 releases publicly (30-day window closes)
- Free tier announcement + purchase link
- Producer post: "Your exclusive window closed — AMBER WEIGHT is your next one."

### June 12 — SIGNAL Pack Release: AMBER WEIGHT (Producer + Architect)

Pack contents:
- 5 bass banks: Sub Foundation, Growl Character, Pluck Stab, Harmonic Drone, Muted Punch
- 1 performance bank: AMBER LIVE — 16 velocity-sensitive pads mapped for live finger-drumming bass lines
- 96 pads total: 3 velocity layers standard, 4 layers on Sub Foundation and Growl Character banks
- Choke groups: Muted Punch pads choke Harmonic Drone pads (monophonic bass feel)
- `MPCE_SETUP.md`: NE = feliX character (bright attack, cut), SW = Oscar character (warm body, sustain), NW = COUPLING (pre-routed for ONSET kick coupling path), SE = SUB (extended low-end layer)

Post title: "AMBER WEIGHT — OBLONG Vol. 1 Is Here (Producer Early Access)"
- Behind-the-scenes post: "How I Designed AMBER WEIGHT" — covers the bass character decision matrix (sub vs. growl vs. pluck), why the LIVE bank has different velocity curves than the other 5, and the OBLONG→ONSET coupling annotation in the pack.
