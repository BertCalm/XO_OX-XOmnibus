### Monthly Patreon Content Cadence (Per Quad)

**Month -1 (before quad launch):**
- Tier 1: "Something is coming" teaser post — no details, one image
- Tier 2: None (engines not yet released)
- Tier 3: Behind-the-scenes journal: "How we designed [engine name] from scratch" (2-3 pages)

**2 weeks before public ship:**
- Tier 2: Full engine access
- Tier 3: 2 Synth Cookbook chapters + 4 Regional Cookbook recipes as early PDF
- Tier 4: Print-on-demand pre-order link for current quad's Regional Cookbook

**Public ship week:**
- Tier 1: Full launch announcement post with Field Guide link
- All tiers: Community challenge launch ("The Competition," "The Session," etc.)

**2 weeks after public ship:**
- Tier 3: Recipe development retrospective — one recipe's full design journey (why the first dish didn't work, what was changed, the accessibility audit results)
- All tiers: Community challenge results

**Month +1 (after quad launch):**
- Tier 3: "Pairs Well With" supplement — 3 coupling patches designed to pair with the current quad's Regional Cookbook meals. "Cook the Sunday Gravy recipe while running this patch."
