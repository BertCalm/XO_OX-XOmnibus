## How to Read This Calendar

Each week has:
- **Primary** -- the main deliverable shipping that week
- **Secondary** -- a smaller release, content drop, or maintenance task
- **Narrative** -- the story being told to the community that week
- **OBRIX Cadence** -- OBRIX-specific content appears every other week minimum (marked with a reef icon)

### Category Key

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `EQ` | Engine Quality -- DSP fixes to raise seance scores |
| `BRIX` | OBRIX flagship content |
| `XPN` | MPC-ready expansion packs |
| `PRESET` | New preset packs for existing engines |
| `GURU` | Guru Bin Transcendental volumes |
| `CONTENT` | Field Guide, site updates, community |
| `FEAT` | Features -- coupling, FX, SDK |

**If V1 launch slips past Week 1:** Compress Weeks 2-4 engine quality work. Vol 1 announcement moves to T+14 from actual launch date. XPN packs shift proportionally.

**If WAV rendering sessions are delayed:** Ship XPN packs as "preset-only" bundles first, add rendered audio in a follow-up patch. Do not hold the pack for audio.

**If Patreon growth is below target (< 20 supporters by Week 8):** Shift Vol 2 content strategy -- release 5 Transcendental presets free as "tasters" alongside the Vol 2 announcement to demonstrate premium value.
