## 4. Cross-Engine Macro Bleed

When 2+ pentagon engines are loaded in XOmnibus, their signature macros subtly influence each other. The influence is ±10-15% parameter nudge — felt but not overpowering. Because that's what families do.

### Bleed Routes

```
                SURFACE
                   │
            ┌──────┼──────┐
            │      │      │
            ▼      │      ▼
     MEDDLING ◄────┼───► DRAMA
         │         │       │
         │    (COMMUNE     │
         │    dampens      │
         │    everything)  │
         ▼                 ▼
        BOND ◄──────────► GROW
```

| Source Macro | Target Macro | Direction | Effect |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| **DRAMA** ↑ | **MEDDLING** ↑ | OLÉ → OHM | Aunts fighting energizes the in-laws — family stress cascades UP |
| **DRAMA** ↑ | **BOND** destabilized | OLÉ → OBBLIGATO | Aunts' fighting stresses the brothers — their relationship gets more volatile |
| **MEDDLING** ↑ | **SURFACE** shifts | OHM → ORPHICA | In-law interference makes the goddess shift her water level — emotional response to family pressure |
| **COMMUNE** ↑ | **DRAMA** ↓ | OHM → OLÉ | Dad's absorption calms the aunts — peace radiates outward |
| **COMMUNE** ↑ | **BOND** stabilized | OHM → OBBLIGATO | Dad's peace calms the sons — parental grounding |
| **COMMUNE** ↑ | **SURFACE** settles | OHM → ORPHICA | Dad's peace settles the wife — the marriage as emotional anchor |
| **GROW** ↑ (teen) | **SIDES** rotation ↑ | OTTONI → OLÉ | Cousins' teen energy excites the aunts' alliance rotation — youth as catalyst |
| **GROW** ↑ (toddler) | **DRAMA** ↓ | OTTONI → OLÉ | The toddler's innocence softens the aunts — everyone loves the baby |
| **BOND** destabilized | **DRAMA** ↑ | OBBLIGATO → OLÉ | Sons fighting stresses the aunts — family mirrors family |
| **SURFACE** extreme | **GROW** shifts | ORPHICA → OTTONI | Mother's emotional state affects how the cousins develop — environment shapes growth |

### The COMMUNE Damper

COMMUNE is special — it's the only macro that **dampens** all other family stress. When the Dad absorbs interference:
- DRAMA decreases (aunts calm down)
- BOND stabilizes (sons find balance)
- MEDDLING decreases (in-laws relax)
- SURFACE settles (wife is at peace)
- GROW proceeds naturally (cousins grow without pressure)

**COMMUNE is the family's emotional immune system.** The Dad's peace heals the whole pentagon.

## 5. Shared Waveguide DNA — Family Physics Engine

The pentagon shares a common waveguide core. Family shares DNA.

### `FamilyWaveguide.h` — Shared DSP Module

```
Shared Core:
├── DelayLine — tunable delay (pitch) with fractional interpolation
├── DampingFilter — lowpass in feedback path (material/air absorption)
├── BodyResonance — coupled resonator for instrument body modeling
├── SympatheticBank — tuned comb filters for sympathetic string/tube vibration
└── OrganicDrift — slow pitch/timing wander for human imperfection

Engine-Specific Exciters (NOT shared):
├── PluckExciter (ORPHICA) — noise burst for harp strings
├── StrumExciter (OLÉ) — multi-string sequential trigger with direction
├── PickExciter (OHM) — fingerstyle/clawhammer attack models
├── AirJetExciter (OBBLIGATO) — filtered noise for flute family
├── ReedExciter (OBBLIGATO) — nonlinear waveshaper for reed family
├── LipBuzzExciter (OTTONI) — lip oscillation for brass
└── BowExciter (OHM) — continuous friction for fiddle
```

**Why this matters:**
- Bug fixes in the waveguide propagate to ALL family engines automatically
- Performance optimizations lift all boats
- The shared physics ensures timbral family resemblance — an XOrphica harp string and an XOhm guitar string feel like they come from the same world
- Narratively: the family literally shares genetic code. Their instruments resonate together because they're built from the same molecules.

## 8. Obed as Hidden Engine Mode — "The Physicist"

**Easter egg:** When XOhm is loaded with MEDDLING=1.0 and COMMUNE=0.0, Obed's FM voice becomes dominant enough to function as a **standalone FM mini-engine**.

**How to discover it:**
- Load XOhm
- Push MEDDLING to maximum
- Pull COMMUNE to zero
- The Dad's organic voice nearly disappears
- Obed's FM takes over — clean, cold, mathematically beautiful
- The parameter panel subtly shifts: the Dad's knobs dim, Obed's FM ratio controls illuminate
- A hidden "PHYSICIST" label appears where "JAM" was

**Obed's FM mini-engine features:**
- 7 atomic ratio presets (Hydrogen through Fission)
- Modulation index control
- Feedback amount (self-modulation)
- Perfect pitch, perfect timing — zero organic drift
- Digital delay + crystalline reverb (his own FX)

**The irony is the point.** The nuclear physicist the in-laws wanted XOrphica to marry — the man they think is better than the hippy Dad — becomes a usable instrument only when the Dad is silenced. Players who find this discover that Obed's FM sounds technically impressive... and emotionally empty. Then they push COMMUNE up and hear what happens when Obed learns to feel. The lesson is built into the DSP.

**Implementation:** Not a separate engine registration — it's a state of XOhm. No additional CPU. Just a UI/behavioral shift when the parameter constellation aligns.

## 10. Build Sequence

Recommended order for developing the pentagon engines:

| Phase | Engine | Rationale |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | **XOhm** | Simplest core voice (physical modeling instruments the user knows well). Establishes the waveguide foundation. FamilyWaveguide.h starts here. |
| 2 | **XOrphica** | Shares waveguide DNA with XOhm (plucked vs picked strings). Adds microsound engine. Marriage coupling can be tested immediately. |
| 3 | **XObbligato** | Adapts waveguide to wind (different exciter, same delay line). Father-son coupling testable. BOND system is self-contained. |
| 4 | **XOttoni** | Adapts wind waveguide to brass (different exciter again). Cousin coupling testable. GROW system builds on BOND's interaction model. |
| 5 | **XOlé** | Most voices (3→6). Alliance system is the most complex interaction model. Built last because it benefits from all prior waveguide/interaction learning. |
| Post | **Ecosystem integration** | Macro bleed, Family Dinner presets, Family Portrait visualization, Obed easter egg |
