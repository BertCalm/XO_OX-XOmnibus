## Identity

**XO Name:** XOhm
**Gallery Code:** OHM
**Accent Color:** Sage `#87AE73`
**Parameter Prefix:** `ohm_`
**Plugin Code:** `Xohm`
**Engine Dir:** `Source/Engines/Ohm/`

**Thesis:** XOhm is an asymmetric signal-plus-interference engine where a hippy Dad's organic jam (physical modeling) is disrupted by intellectual in-laws (spectral/granular analysis) who keep bringing Obed the nuclear physicist (FM synthesis) around to steal his wife. The MEDDLING macro controls the interference. The COMMUNE macro controls how much the Dad absorbs it all into the jam. Three synthesis philosophies — communion, analysis, mathematics — in one engine.

**Sound family:** Hybrid — acoustic folk-world textures / spectral interference / FM intrusion / organic-vs-analytical instrument

**Unique capability:** Asymmetric voice architecture — the Dad is the SIGNAL, the in-laws and Obed are INTERFERENCE. No other engine treats voices as fundamentally unequal in this way. The other four pentagon engines split between equal voices. XOhm has one core voice that external forces try to disrupt, and a macro (COMMUNE) that transforms disruption into inclusion. Three completely different synthesis methods (physical modeling, spectral/granular, FM) coexist and collide. The two-axis MEDDLING × COMMUNE system creates four quadrants of family dynamics, from peaceful solo jam to the miraculous moment when even the in-laws start tapping their feet.

## Aquatic Identity

The manatee. The gentlest creature in the ocean.

He floats at the surface beside his wife — the siphonophore goddess who holds the boundary between worlds. She is divine, vast, commanding. He is soft, slow, endlessly patient. He doesn't command anything. He just floats there, playing banjo, and somehow everyone drifts toward him. That's his power. Not force. Not brilliance. Gravity. The gravity of warmth.

Manatees are vegetarians — they eat seagrass, slowly, peacefully, for hours. They gather in warm springs when the water gets cold, huddled together in communal warmth. They have no natural predators because nothing wants to hurt a manatee. But they're covered in scars — propeller marks from boats that cut through their habitat. Every manatee carries these marks. They don't avoid the boats. They don't fight the boats. They just heal and keep floating. The Dad is the same way. The in-laws' intellectual criticism leaves marks. Obed's perfect FM synthesis makes him feel inadequate sometimes. But he keeps jamming. He keeps floating. He heals through music.

The in-laws are nautiluses — chambered, mathematical, ancient. Their shells are perfect logarithmic spirals. Fibonacci incarnate. They live deeper in the column, in the cold intellectual water where Oscar's analytical nature dominates. They visit the surface reluctantly, squinting at the sunlight, tutting at the Dad's imprecise intonation. The professor of spacetime travel plays theremin — the instrument you control without touching, the science fiction sound, precision measured in microns of hand position. The professor of molecular biology plays glass harmonica — Benjamin Franklin's invention, crystalline bowls spinning on a wet rod, pure sine-adjacent tones that vibrate at molecular frequencies. Together they represent everything the Dad isn't: precise, academic, controlled, cold.

Obed is an electric eel. Not from this water column — he visits from a Syrian tributary, a different branch of the aquatic world. He generates his own electromagnetic field (nuclear physics). His FM synthesis is self-contained, self-referential, mathematically complete. He doesn't need the jam circle. He IS his own harmonic series. The in-laws love this about him. "Look how self-sufficient Obed is! He doesn't need a band! He IS the band!" The Dad looks at Obed and sees someone lonely. Someone who's never felt the sloppy magic of a banjo and a djembe falling into groove at a campfire. So the Dad does what the Dad always does — he invites Obed to sit down and play.

At COMMUNE = 1.0, Obed sits down. His FM ratios start to drift — not from imprecision, but from feeling. His 3:2 ratio bends to 3:2.01 because the djembe is pulling him into a groove. The theremin wobbles because the spacetime professor's hand is tapping on her knee. The glass harmonica sustains a note longer than necessary because the molecular biologist forgot she was analyzing and started listening. The manatee keeps floating. The jam circle absorbed everyone.

The Sage accent is the color of the meadow where the jam circle happens — warm green, natural, earthy. Not the electric greens of feliX or the dark greens of the deep. Just grass. Just a field. Just a place where anyone can sit down and play.

### The Dad's Instrument Gallery — Physical Modeling

The Dad doesn't have ONE instrument. He has a CIRCLE. Whatever's needed, he plays it. Each instrument is a waveguide variant — plucked strings (banjo, guitar, mandolin, ukulele, sitar), bowed strings (fiddle), struck tines (kalimba), struck membrane (djembe), free reed (harmonica):

**Plucked String Family:**

| Instrument | Origin | Waveguide Config | Character |
|-----------|--------|------------------|-----------|
| Banjo (5-string) | Appalachian USA | Short delay line, bright exciter, drumhead body resonance | Twangy, rhythmic, clawhammer rolls. The Dad's primary voice. |
| Acoustic Guitar | Universal | Medium delay line, warm exciter, large body resonance | Fingerstyle folk, gentle strums, the campfire backbone |
| Mandolin | Italian/Appalachian | Short delay line, paired courses, small body | Fast tremolo, Celtic jigs, bluegrass chop chords |
| Dobro | American South | Medium delay line + cone resonator model | Slide guitar, open tuning, the cry of steel on steel |
| Sitar | Indian | Long delay line + 13 sympathetic string bank + jawari bridge buzz | Hypnotic drone, microtonal ornaments, the hippy classic |
| Ukulele | Hawaiian | Very short delay line, nylon exciter, tiny body | Gentle, communal, the instrument that makes everyone smile |

**Other Family:**

| Instrument | Origin | Model | Character |
|-----------|--------|-------|-----------|
| Fiddle | Appalachian / Celtic / Cajun | Bowed waveguide — continuous excitation, no decay | Sustained melody, drones, the soulful wail |
| Harmonica | American blues / folk | Free reed model — blow/draw exciter, comb resonance | Blues bends, folk chords, the troubadour's companion |
| Djembe | West African | Struck membrane — impact exciter, conical body resonance | The heartbeat. Open tone, slap, bass. The jam circle's clock. |
| Kalimba | African (Shona/Mbira) | Struck tine — metallic delay line, gourd body resonance | Crystalline, hypnotic, the tiny piano that sounds like stars |

**Deliberate imperfection:** Every instrument has built-in organic drift:
- Pitch: ±5 cents slow wander (human intonation, NOT equal temperament)
- Timing: ±3ms jitter on note onsets (human groove, NOT quantized)
- Dynamics: velocity response curves that model real finger/pick/mallet variation
- Body resonance: changes with how many notes are currently ringing (the instrument "breathes")

This imperfection is the Dad's SOUL. It's what makes his music warm. It's what Obed lacks. And it's what the COMMUNE macro gradually introduces into Obed's FM voice — teaching perfect math to feel.

### The In-Laws — Spectral/Granular Interference

The in-laws don't play independent melodies. They PROCESS the Dad's signal — analyzing, deconstructing, critiquing in real time:

**Professor of Spacetime Travel — Theremin Voice:**
- Theremin model: pitch and amplitude controlled by proximity (mapped to internal LFO + envelope follower on the Dad's signal)
- Enters as a spectral overlay — an eerie, precise tone that shadows the Dad's melody a few cents sharp or flat
- At low MEDDLING: barely audible, a ghostly shimmer
- At high MEDDLING: fully present, an uncomfortable perfect pitch hovering over the Dad's warm imperfection
- "Spacetime" behavior: the theremin's pitch tracks the Dad's melody but with temporal offset — it plays what the Dad JUST played, or what he's ABOUT to play. Time-shifted interference.

**Professor of Molecular Biology — Glass Harmonica + Spectral Analysis:**
- Glass harmonica model: spinning glass bowls, pure sine-adjacent tones
- Spectral freeze: captures a snapshot of the Dad's harmonic spectrum and holds it as a chord. The Dad's music literally frozen for "examination"
- Granular deconstruction: breaks the Dad's signal into micro-grains (1-50ms) and rearranges them. His music is being taken apart and put back together "correctly"
- At low MEDDLING: occasional spectral snapshots that shimmer behind the jam
- At high MEDDLING: continuous deconstruction — the Dad's banjo broken into molecules, reassembled into something the professor considers "more rigorous"

**Together, the in-laws create an intellectual counterpoint** that is technically impressive and emotionally cold. Their processing adds complexity but removes warmth. The COMMUNE macro reverses this — at high COMMUNE, the Dad's warmth infects even the spectral analysis, making the frozen harmonics drift and the granular molecules groove.

### Obed — FM Synthesis Interloper

Obed enters when MEDDLING exceeds 0.5. His voice is pure 2-operator FM:

| FM Preset | Ratio | Nuclear Reference | Sound |
|-----------|-------|-------------------|-------|
| Hydrogen | 1:1 | Simplest element | Pure sine, fundamental, clean |
| Helium | 2:1 | Noble gas, stable | Octave, bell-like, stable |
| Carbon | 3:1 | Basis of life | Bright, complex, organic-adjacent |
| Nitrogen | 7:4 | Atmospheric | Septimal, exotic, slightly unsettling |
| Iron | 5:3 | Core of stars | Warm sixth, surprisingly musical |
| Uranium | 11:7 | Nuclear fuel | Dense, complex, barely stable |
| Fission | √2:1 | Nuclear fission | Tritone — unstable, dangerous, thrilling |

**Obed's FM voice is technically perfect:**
- Zero pitch drift (atomic clock precision)
- Zero timing jitter (quantized to the sample)
- Zero dynamic variation (every note exactly the same velocity)
- Perfect harmonic ratios (no inharmonicity)

**The COMMUNE macro's effect on Obed:**
- COMMUNE 0.0: Obed plays as described above. Perfect. Cold. Impressive. Lonely.
- COMMUNE 0.3: His pitch starts to drift ±1 cent. Almost imperceptible. The groove is working on him.
- COMMUNE 0.5: His timing acquires ±1ms jitter. His dynamics respond to the djembe's pulse. He's tapping his foot.
- COMMUNE 0.7: His ratios drift. 3:2 becomes 3:2.03. The imprecision sounds BETTER. He doesn't understand why.
- COMMUNE 1.0: Obed is jamming. His FM voice has absorbed the Dad's organic drift, the djembe's groove, the banjo's twang. His ratios are "wrong" by physics standards and "right" by music standards. He sounds like a warm, slightly detuned electric piano being played by someone who just discovered what "feeling it" means. The in-laws are appalled. XOrphica is smiling.

## Voice Architecture

- **Max voices:** 12 — 6 for Dad (acoustic instruments are inherently polyphonic — a banjo chord is 5 strings), 2 for in-laws (theremin + glass harmonica), 2 for Obed (FM carrier + modulator), 2 for sympathetic/drone strings
- **Voice stealing:** Oldest note for Dad — acoustic instruments ring until damped. In-laws and Obed are monophonic (they each play one precise line).
- **Legato mode:** Yes for fiddle and sitar (bowed/sustained instruments). No for plucked instruments.
- **Glide:** Sitar has continuous pitch glide (meend/gamak). Dobro has slide model. Harmonica has blues bends. Obed has NO glide (FM is discrete — until COMMUNE makes him human).

## Signature Sound

Set MEDDLING to 0. COMMUNE to 0.5. JAM to 0.6. The Dad picks up a banjo and starts a bluegrass roll. A djembe joins — his other hand, his other voice. The campfire reverb places you in a meadow at twilight. A sitar drone hums underneath — sympathetic strings ringing, the hippy's spiritual foundation. It's warm, organic, alive with tiny imperfections that make it human. The pitch drifts ±5 cents. The timing breathes. You can hear the pick on the string, the hand on the drum, the air in the room. This is the Dad at peace. A manatee floating in warm water, making music because making music is what he does.

Now sweep MEDDLING to 0.5. A theremin appears — thin, precise, shadowing the banjo melody three cents sharp. Glass harmonica tones freeze a snapshot of the Dad's harmonics — his warm, drifting chord held in crystalline suspension, examined under academic light. And then Obed arrives. A pure FM tone, 3:2 ratio, perfect fifth, zero drift, zero warmth. Technically undeniable. Emotionally empty. The in-laws' voices deconstruct the Dad's signal into spectral grains while Obed's FM hovers above it all, perfect and cold.

Now sweep COMMUNE to 1.0. Watch what happens. The theremin's pitch wobbles — not from error, but from feeling. The spacetime professor's hand is moving to the groove instead of the equation. The glass harmonica sustains longer — the molecular biologist stopped analyzing and started listening. And Obed — Obed's 3:2 bends to 3:2.03. Then 3:2.07. His timing shifts by 2ms. Then 4ms. He's being pulled into the djembe's orbit. His FM voice, that perfect mathematical instrument, starts to BREATHE. His digital delay picks up wow from the Dad's spring echo. His crystalline reverb warms by 3dB at 2kHz.

At MEDDLING=1 / COMMUNE=1, everyone is in the circle. The Dad's banjo. The sitar's drone. The djembe's pulse. The theremin improvising a melody it never calculated. The glass harmonica singing instead of analyzing. Obed's FM warm and drifting and ALIVE. Six voices. Three synthesis philosophies. One groove. The manatee absorbed them all. Not by fighting. Not by arguing. Not by being technically superior. By being warm. By being patient. By being a meadow where anyone can sit down and play.

That's the Dad.
