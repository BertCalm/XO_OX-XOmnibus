## Overview

XOxytocin is a circuit-modeling synthesizer where Sternberg's three components of love — Intimacy (thermal resistance), Passion (voltage drive), and Commitment (capacitance) — interact to produce 8 emergent circuit topologies. Note duration is a primary performance parameter: staccato produces infatuation states (bright, harsh, unstable); legato produces consummate love (warm, full, resonant). A Remember mode accumulates relationship state across a session, making the circuit evolve over time.

Circuit lineages: RE-201 warmth (Intimacy), MS-20 saturation (Passion), Moog 4-pole ladder (Commitment), Serge cross-modulation architecture. 26 parameters, `oxy_` prefix. TriangularCoupling (proposed 16th type).

## 2. Market Positioning

### Where It Sits

XOxytocin occupies a three-way intersection that is genuinely uncrowded: **vintage analog circuit modeling** (crowded), **temporal synthesis where note duration drives timbre** (nearly empty), and **session-accumulating state** (nobody has this). The overlap of all three is unoccupied market space.

The nearest commercial comparisons:

| Product | Company | Overlap | Difference |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minimoog V | Arturia | Moog ladder resonance | Static. No thermal model, no love topology, no temporal duration mechanic. |
| MS-20 V | Arturia | Sallen-Key filter, self-oscillation | One voice, no emotional state accumulation, no cross-modulation of multiple forces. |
| Tape Delay | Various | RE-201 warmth | FX only. No synthesis engine underneath the warmth. |
| DIVA | u-he | Circuit component modeling | DIVA models specific circuits accurately. XOxytocin models circuit *relationships*. Fundamentally different design objective. |
| Repro-5 | u-he | Analog warmth, component aging | Aging is aesthetic approximation, not session-state accumulation. No duration-as-timbre. |
| Model D | Arturia | Moog emulation | Single-circuit identity. XOxytocin merges three legendary circuits simultaneously. |
| Phase Plant | Kilohearts | Circuit-adjacent saturation | Modular, not character. No mythology. No emotional arc. |

### Genuine Differentiators

1. **Note duration as the primary timbral parameter** — no mainstream synthesizer does this. Buchla instruments explored it in hardware, but no VST/AU implements it as a core identity mechanic.
2. **Three-legend circuit fusion** — RE-201, MS-20, and Moog Ladder exist simultaneously in one engine, interacting rather than switching.
3. **Session-accumulating state** — the circuit remembers a session. No other synthesizer on the market learns your playing habits and changes its character accordingly.
4. **Love topology as circuit topology** — Sternberg's 8 love types are not metadata or naming conventions. They are different circuit states with measurably different audio characters.

### Is "Love as Synthesis" a Compelling Marketing Angle?

**Verdict: Yes, with calibration.**

The risk is that technical users read "love" and assume soft, unserious, or limited. The mitigation is leading with the circuit credentials: *RE-201 × MS-20 × Moog Ladder in one engine.* The love framework is then introduced as the mechanism — not the genre target. "It's three legendary circuits falling in love" is a hook that works for technical users (because the circuits are real and respected) and casual users (because the metaphor is immediate and human).

The danger zone is marketing directly at the love framework without anchoring the circuit pedigree first. Metal/Industrial at 4 stars confirms the concept crosses into genres where "love" as framing should be entirely invisible — it's the sonic result that matters.

## 3. Feature Priority — Ruthless Cut

### The Parameters That Earn Their Place (Keep, No Question)

| Parameter | Why It's Essential |
|---|---|
| `oxy_intimacy` | Core force 1. Defines the entire thermal warmth system. Non-negotiable. |
| `oxy_passion` | Core force 2. MS-20 saturation driver. Non-negotiable. |
| `oxy_commitment` | Core force 3. Moog depth and resonance lock. Non-negotiable. |
| `oxy_warmth_rate` | The sonic identity is temporal dynamics. A thermal rate without user control defeats the concept. Keep. |
| `oxy_passion_rate` | Same logic. Fast/slow passion response is audibly different and dramatically important. Keep. |
| `oxy_commit_rate` | Critical for distinguishing infatuation from consummate love. Keep. |
| `oxy_cross_mod` | The Serge-inspired circular routing is the most novel DSP element. The engine is significantly less interesting without it. Keep. |
| `oxy_circuit_age` | Built-in degradation is a headline feature. Keep. |
| `oxy_remember` | Session accumulation is the most original mechanic. Keep as toggle. |
| `oxy_feedback` | Circuit reinforcement directly affects love-state stability vs. instability. Keep. |
| `oxy_cutoff` | Filter is modulated by love state — producers need baseline control. Keep. |
| `oxy_attack` / `oxy_decay` / `oxy_sustain` / `oxy_release` | Standard ADSR. Keep. Non-negotiable for playability. |
| `oxy_voices` | Polyphony control needed. Keep. |
| `oxy_output` | Master level. Keep. |
| `oxy_velocity_curve` | D001 compliance and expressive variation. Keep. |

### Cut or Merge (11 parameters that underperform)

| Parameter | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `oxy_lfo_rate` | **MERGE into macro MOVEMENT** | An LFO rate without explicit modulation destination clarity is just another rate knob. Expose through M2 (MOVEMENT) macro rather than as a standalone parameter. Keep as a mod matrix destination but de-emphasize as a front-panel control. |
| `oxy_lfo_depth` | **MERGE/HIDE** | Same logic. LFO depth matters only when LFO rate is meaningful. Pair these as a single MOVEMENT macro control. |
| `oxy_lfo_shape` | **KEEP but HIDE** | Five LFO shapes are genuinely useful but not a front-panel priority. Put in an advanced panel or secondary controls layer. |
| `oxy_mod_wheel` | **RENAME and CLARIFY** | "Mod Wheel Dest" is a routing option, not a sound parameter. This should be labeled clearly as a performance routing selector and moved to a performance/MIDI section. Its presence in the main parameter list inflates the count. |
| `oxy_aftertouch` | **SAME as above** | Move to performance section. Keep the functionality; remove it from the main parameter page. |
| `oxy_pitch` | **STANDARD** | Every synth has this. Keep but it's not a differentiator. |
| `oxy_detune` | **STANDARD** | Keep for voice spread. Not a differentiator. |
| `oxy_pan` | **STANDARD** | Keep. Not a differentiator. |

### The Missing Parameter (Add This)

**`oxy_topology_lock`** — a toggle or selector that locks the circuit to a specific love topology (e.g., Infatuation, Romantic, Companionate, Consummate) regardless of how it's played. Currently, the only way to access a specific topology is through note duration, which is a performance mechanic. For DAW-based producers doing MIDI programming, they need a way to set the love state statically. Without this, XOxytocin cannot be fully used in non-real-time production workflows. This is a critical accessibility gap.

**`oxy_transition_shape`** — controls how the circuit transitions between love states (linear, exponential, sigmoid). The S-curve transition is more emotionally realistic than linear, and producers need control over the feel of the arc.

### Honest Parameter Count Assessment

After cuts and merges: the **core front-panel parameter set is approximately 18-20**. The remaining 6-8 are performance/routing/standard controls that belong in an advanced section. This is a cleaner instrument presentation than 26 equal-weight knobs and matches how producers actually interact with a synth.

## 4. Preset Strategy

### Does the 8-Love-Type Organization Work?

**Partially.** The 8-love-type taxonomy (Non-Love, Liking, Infatuation, Empty, Romantic, Companionate, Fatuous, Consummate) is intellectually coherent but produces a browsing experience that requires producer education. Producers browsing presets think in sonic terms, not psychological taxonomy.

**Recommended dual-organization:**
- **Primary browse**: Sonic descriptor categories (Warm Pads, Harsh Leads, Deep Bass, Evolving Textures, Ambient Drones)
- **Secondary tag**: Love type (for users who learn the system)
- The love type should appear as a tag on each preset, not as the top-level folder name

### Genre-Specific Presets Required at Launch

These are non-negotiable for day-one reviews and social media content:

| Preset Name | Love State | Genre | Why Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| First Warmth | Companionate | Lo-fi / Neo-Soul | Demonstrator of the thermal bloom mechanic |

| Static Discharge | Fatuous | Industrial / Metal | High passion, low commitment — unstable and angular |
| Ghost Memory | Liking | Film Score | Remember mode on, barely-there warmth |

| The Lure | Infatuation | Synthwave | Bright bioluminescent attack, slow warmth fade |
| Lifespan | Companionate → Consummate | Experimental | Designed to evolve across a full session with Remember mode |

### Relationship Arc Presets

**Yes — make 3-5 of these and market them explicitly.** These are presets where Remember mode is on by default, circuit age is moderate, and the commit rate is set to maximum. They are designed to sound different in minute one versus minute ten. This is a category of preset that does not exist anywhere in the synth market. Call them "Arc presets" or "Session presets." These become the engine's headline demo content.

### Minimum Preset Count

**120 presets minimum** (XO_OX standard). Recommended launch target **140 presets** given the 8-topology architecture provides natural organizational structure.

Proposed distribution:
- Non-Love / Liking: 15 (init state variants, cold circuits)
- Infatuation: 20 (bright, harsh, attack-forward)
- Romantic: 20 (warm + drive balance)
- Companionate: 25 (warm, stable, deep — the lo-fi/neo-soul core)
- Consummate: 20 (maximum all-three engagement)
- Fatuous: 10 (high passion, unstable commitment)
- Empty Love: 5 (haunting, commitment-only, no warmth or drive)
- Session Arc presets: 5 (Remember mode on, evolving over time)
- Total: 120

## 5. Competitive Edge

**XOxytocin is the only synthesizer on the market where how long you press a key determines the emotional character of the sound — not just its amplitude envelope — because the circuit has a relationship with time that behaves differently depending on whether you're playing staccato infatuation or legato consummate love, and that relationship accumulates across an entire session via a circuit that remembers you.**

No Serum preset, Vital patch, or Phase Plant module does this. Not because of lack of DSP sophistication — but because no other synthesizer was designed around the idea that *note duration is emotional content.*

## 6. Product Manager Summary

- **SHIP.** The concept clears every relevant bar: 14 of 25 genre specialists at 4+ stars, 6 at maximum excitement, genuine market whitespace identified, no direct competitor, doctrines pre-checked and passing. The engine addresses three gaps simultaneously (circuit modeling, temporal-duration synthesis, session-state accumulation) that are all empty in the 71-engine fleet.

- **Biggest risk**: The Remember mode and relationship arc — which are the most original mechanics — require producer education that most will not do. If the first-use experience (no Remember mode, no relationship arc, just cold circuit) is not immediately compelling, producers stop there and never discover the depth. The init patch and out-of-box playability must be excellent. This is the build-phase critical path: make the circuit sound interesting before any of the temporal mechanics engage.

- **Biggest opportunity**: The "note duration = emotional character" mechanic is a genuine first in commercial synthesis. This is a YouTube and social media demo that writes itself — play staccato and then slowly hold the same note into a legato sustain, and the audience hears the circuit fall in love in real time. This is a content flywheel. One demo video captures every genre target simultaneously.

- **Recommended launch strategy**: Lead with sonic credentials (RE-201 × MS-20 × Moog Ladder), reveal the love framework as the *mechanism*, not the *genre target*. Market the "relationship arc" presets as a separate product feature — "the synth that evolves with your session" is a different and more accessible pitch than "love theory in circuit modeling." Position alongside neo-soul, lo-fi, and cinematic as the primary genre landing zones. Let the industrial and experimental communities discover the MS-20 extreme on their own — they will.

- **One unresolved gap that must be fixed before build**: Add `oxy_topology_lock` (static love-type selection for DAW producers who cannot use note-duration performance mechanic). Without it, XOxytocin is not usable in non-real-time MIDI programming workflows. This is a P0 usability requirement.
