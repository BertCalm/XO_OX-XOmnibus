# Water Quad — Concept Brief

**Status:** Early concept
**Theme:** Water in four states — four time scales of transformation
**Naming convention:** All "XOver-" prefix (consistent suite identity)

---

## The Quad

| Engine | Concept | Quadrant | Prefix | Time Scale |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|------------|
| **XOverwash** | Watercolor diffusion — one drop bleeds and dilutes | NE | `wash_` | Seconds |
| **XOverworn** | Erosion — accumulated repetition reshapes the sound | SW | `worn_` | Session-long |
| **XOverflow** | Steam/pressure — energy builds until valves burst | NW | `flow_` | Phrases |
| **XOvercast** | Supercooled crystallization — one tap triggers phase change | SE | `cast_` | Instant |

## Design Principles

- Four "Over-" words, four water states, four quadrants, four time scales
- Parameter prefixes are all four letters, all evocative
- Each engine embodies a different relationship between water and time
- **XOverwash** — diffusion over seconds (watercolor bleeding)
- **XOverworn** — erosion over a session (water shaping rock through repetition)
- **XOverflow** — pressure building over phrases (steam until burst)
- **XOvercast** — instant phase change (supercooled crystallization on contact)
