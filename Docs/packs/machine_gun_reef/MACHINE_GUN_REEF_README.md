## Programs

### 1. Incoming (ONSET)
ONSET's full synthesis drum kit at maximum aggression. All 8 voices — kick, snare, hats, clap, tom, perc, FX — velocity-layered for dynamic response. XVC cross-voice coupling makes your kick tighten the snare automatically.

This is the Machine Gun: fast, punchy, relentless.

### 2. The Reef (OCEANIC)
OCEANIC's bioluminescent swarm synthesis mapped across 4 octaves. Low register delivers thick sub-bass with swarming movement. Mid register gives you harmonic cluster tones that shift as you sustain. High register: shimmer and scatter.

Use the COUPLING macro to route OCEANIC's swarm movement into ONSET's machine gun rhythm.

### 3. Apex (ORCA)
ORCA's predator architecture: wavetable scanning, echolocation echo feedback, and breach dynamics. The HUNT macro activates velocity-sensitive filter sweeps — play harder, open wider. High velocities trigger breach transients that punch through any mix.

This is the hunter of the reef.

### 4. Pressure (OBESE)
OBESE delivers the weight. Sub-heavy foundation bass with the Mojo axis running from pure analog warmth (pad 1) to crisp digital precision (pad 8). Pads 9–12 are arpeggiator patterns — rhythmic bass lines you can play with one finger. Pads 13–16 are character extremes.

This is what keeps everything grounded.

### 5. Full Reef (All Engines)
16 curated pads — the best of all four engines in one program. Kick, snare, reef bass, swarm texture, orca lead, pressure sub, chromatophore shimmer, breach impact. A complete production toolkit that works immediately.
