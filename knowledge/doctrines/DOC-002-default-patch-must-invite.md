# DOC-002: The Default Patch is the First Impression

**Ghosts**: Pearlman, Kakehashi, Vangelis, Tomita
**Engine**: OBRIX (2026-03-19)

The sound that greets a musician when they first press a key determines whether they trust the instrument. Default patches must:
- Use a waveform with harmonic content the filter can sculpt (Saw, not Sine)
- Have at least one modulation route already active (e.g., LFO → FilterCutoff)
- Sound good without touching anything

"If the 30-second test fails, the instrument fails." — Distilled from Kakehashi's 808 principle.
