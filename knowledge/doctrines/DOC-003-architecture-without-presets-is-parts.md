# DOC-003: Architecture Without Presets is Parts on the Floor

**Ghosts**: Smith, Kakehashi, Vangelis
**Engine**: OBRIX (2026-03-19)

An engine with brilliant architecture and zero presets cannot demonstrate what it is. Presets are not afterthoughts — they are the instrument's vocabulary. Minimum viable preset library:
- 7 presets showing core moods
- At minimum 3 presets with aftertouch already wired (for expressive instruments)
- For engines with coupling: at least 2 presets named for coupling partner ("OBRIX+ONSET", "OBRIX+OPAL")

Do not ship an engine as "complete" until it has a starter preset library.
