# BLS-003: OBRIX COUPLING Macro — Ecosystem Citizenship Parameter

**Ghost**: Smith
**Engine**: OBRIX (2026-03-19)

Dave Smith blessed the COUPLING macro's conceptual design: a single parameter scaling sensitivity across all 13 coupling types, allowing a musician to dial their citizenship in the XOceanus ecosystem. "OBRIX+ONSET coupling creates a drum-FM'd bass. OBRIX+OPAL creates granular ring mod. These are not preset tricks — these are new instruments created at runtime by coupling state." The COUPLING macro is a design choice worth protecting and making more visible. Future presets should be named for their coupling states to make this architecture discoverable.
