# VIS-002: Coupling Macro Should Work Without Partner Engines

**Ghost**: Kakehashi, Smith
**Engine**: OBRIX (2026-03-19)

A COUPLING macro that does nothing audible when no partner engine is loaded is a macro that frustrates beginners. Solution: at low values, COUPLING creates internal self-routing — gentle shimmer or chorus between active bricks within the engine itself. At high values, it opens full cross-engine coupling sensitivity. This makes the macro always expressive, graduating from internal to external coupling naturally.
