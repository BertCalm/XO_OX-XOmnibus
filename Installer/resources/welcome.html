Welcome to XOceanus

by XO_OX Designs

Version 1.0.0

XOceanus is a free, open-source multi-engine synthesizer platform by XO_OX Designs. It merges 73 character instruments into one unified creative environment where engines couple, collide, and mutate into sounds impossible with any single synthesizer.

Whether you are composing in a DAW or performing standalone, XOceanus gives you a full fleet of synthesis engines — from tuned percussion and vocal synthesis to cellular automata and circuit-modeled analog — all connected through a real-time cross-engine coupling matrix.

This installer includes

  • AU Plugin — XOceanus.component for Logic Pro, Ableton Live, Bitwig, and any AU-compatible DAW
  • Standalone Application — XOceanus.app for DAW-free use
  • Factory Presets — 15 mood categories with thousands of ready-to-play patches

Learn more at xo-ox.org
